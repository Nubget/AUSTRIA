Wiener Schnitzel
first attested in 1845.

Thin meat fried in fat. The meat is usually thinned by pounding with a meat tenderizer. Most commonly, the meats are breaded before frying. The breaded schnitzel is popular in many countries and made using veal, pork, chicken, mutton, beef, or turkey.

Germknödel
Served a a dessert and a main cousre.

A fluffy yeast dough dumpling filled with spiced plum jam and served with melted butter, a mix of poppy seeds and sugar and sometimes is served with vanilla cream sauce.
